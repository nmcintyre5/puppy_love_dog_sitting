Welcome to Puppy Love Dog Sitting, where your furry friends receive top-notch care from highly experienced and dedicated dog lovers. We understand the importance of leaving your pets in good hands, which is why we treat them as if they were our own.

Though we miss our beloved black lab mix dearly, our home is filled with Rover love until we are ready for another furry companion. When you leave your pet with us, expect continuous communication and plenty of adorable photos to keep you updated during your time away.

Our ideal match is a friendly and loving furry companion who gets along well with humans and other dogs. While we have a soft spot for golden retrievers and labs, we welcome all breeds with open arms.

Amenities & Environment

Our Typical Routine:

We both work from home full-time, so we're basically always available to shower your pets with attention and care. Our typical routine is:

  • 7:30am Morning Walk
  • 8:00am Breakfast
  • 12:00pm Afternoon Walk
  • 5:00pm Evening Walk
  • 5:30pm Dinner
  • 10:00pm Potty Break

Our Space:

We're equipped with all the essentials to make your dogs feel right at home. We have:

  • Large dog bed at the foot of our bed.
  • Food & water bowls.
  • Baby carrots for treats.
  • Security cameras.
  • A baby gate for separate feedings.
  • A large basket of toys.

Our Safety Measures:

To ensure a safe and comfortable environment, we do meetings in neutral spaces and separate feedings when we have more than one dog with us. They are always carefully monitored, and we have a baby gate to keep them separate as needed (mostly in case of too much fun). To ensure a harmonious pack, we kindly ask that all dogs be spayed or neutered.

Requirements

  • Friendly with all humans, both male and female.
  • Friendly with other dogs.
  • Must be over 1 year old
  • Must be spayed/neutered.
  • Must be okay to be home alone for an hour or two. They will never be alone for more than 4 hours at a time.
  • Oral meds are okay. We do not have experience with injectable meds.

We invite you to entrust us with your furry family members, knowing that we'll go above and beyond to create an experience they'll wag their tails about. Please message us to discuss your canine's needs. We can't wait to welcome your four-legged friends into our loving home!

Information We Need

Please let us know what your typical routine is (feeding, exercise, and bathroom breaks) and if your dog has any special needs or behaviors that we should be aware of, including any anxiety, reactivity, medications, or dietary restrictions. By having this information, we can create a safe and enjoyable experience for your pet.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should we pack?

We recommend packing your pet's essentials, including food, treats, and any medications they may need. Feel free to bring along anything else that will make your pet feel comfortable during their stay.

  • Food
  • Poo bags
  • Leash/harness
  • Crate, if crate trained
  • Meds, if applicable

Optional:

  • Food/water bowls
  • Treats
  • Dog bed / blanket
  • Toys

How do we book with you?

To book with us, simply send us an email at puppylove@kurmasana.com with your desired dates and any questions you may have. We'll get back to you as soon as possible to confirm your booking and discuss further details.

How many dogs do you host at once?

We can host up to two dogs at a time to ensure they receive personalized attention and care. If you have multiple dogs, feel free to inquire about availability and accommodations.

Do you send photos?

Yes, we understand how important it is to stay connected with your furry friend while you're away. We'll send you regular photo updates of your pet having a great time during their stay with us.

What time are you available for drop off or pickup?

We warmly welcome drop-offs between 7:00 AM and 10:00 PM. Should your travel plans extend beyond these hours, we kindly request that you reserve an extra night with us.
